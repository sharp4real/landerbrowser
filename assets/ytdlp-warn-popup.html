Before You Use Video Downloader
Lander Browser's Video Downloader uses yt-dlp, an open-source tool that can download videos from many websites.

Please read before continuing:
• Downloading videos may violate the Terms of Service of the platform you're downloading from (e.g. YouTube, Twitch).
• Only download content you have the right to download — content you own, content licensed for download, or content in the public domain.
• Do not use this tool to download copyrighted material without permission.
• Lander Browser is not responsible for how you use yt-dlp.
