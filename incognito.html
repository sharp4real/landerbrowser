What incognito does and doesn't do
  • History, cookies, and form data are cleared when you close this window.
  • Downloads and bookmarks you create are kept.
  • Your activity is not invisible — your ISP, employer, or websites can still see your traffic.
  • Your IP address is unchanged. Use a VPN for stronger anonymity.
